PASSAGGI FESTIVAL

POSTER DESIGN

Visual identity for Passaggi Festival 2021. Passaggi is a non-fiction festival held every year in the city of Fano (PU). The theme of 2021's edition was "Infinity and Provisional".

The ambivalent title has been visually translated into a paper tightrope walker, poised on a rope, which symbolizes the indeterminacy and the provisionality of the post-covid period. The butterfly wings, instead, represent a reference to Dante Alighieri for the 700th anniversary of his death, and they symbolize the infinite desire of freedom from this provisional balance in which we live all together after the pandemic situation.
