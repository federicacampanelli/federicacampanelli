KINETIC TYPOGRAPHY

MOTION GRAPHIC

Expressiveness brought by kinetic typography is explored in this extract from the italian audiobook Alice in Wonderland read by Aldo Busi. The extract is about the moment when Alice meets the Caterpillar who tells her the magical effects of the mushroom on which he is sitting.
