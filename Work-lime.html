BRANDING

EDITORIAL

The agency Aboca always had difficulties to communicate and relate with a young target. What could bring young people closer to values like health and nature care? Here it comes LIME, a new unconventional and multi-sensorial brand that through a festival and a bi-monthly folding brochure, gives the chance to rediscover values such as bodycare, well-being and sustainable life. The festival itself and all the visual communication of the brand are linked to the five senses and the theme of sensoriality.

Five brochures, one for each sense (sight, hearing, taste, smell, touch) in which people can find issues concerning physical and psychological health. Articles are positioned on the front of the brochure while posters on the back.

All the informations about the project, the events and the cities in which the festival is held can be found inside the website.

PROJECT DETAILS ↓

Client: Aboca
Year: 2016
Designed with: Veronica Cecchini and Edoardo Jorge Beltrammi
Brochure Format: open 70x50cm, closed 25x17,5cm (printed with offset)
Font: Graphik, Sporting Grotesque
Project made during the Communication design course at UNIRSM Design
Project supervisors: prof. Lucia Roscini, Andrea Medri.
